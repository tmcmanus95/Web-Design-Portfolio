About Me

Welcome to my website! As a web developer with a background in care work and behavioral therapy with adults with developmental disabilities, I have seen first-hand the impact that a more accessible internet can have on the lives of disabled and neurodivergent populations. Whether it be creating sleek, simple applications that everyone can use, or adapting websites to meet the needs of all regardless of their abilities or disabilities, I strive to provide the highest quality web design available.

Through using cutting edge frameworks, languages, and toolkits, I plan to create several applications that will cater to the needs of the populations that I work with. Driven by curiosity and a commitment to growth and accessibility, I continuously explore new technologies and design trends, seeking opportunities to collaborate and contribute to projects that prioritize inclusivity and promote the pushing beyond the cutting edge of innovation. When the internet is accessible to all, it is functioning at it's highest potential. And until it is, we are all falling short. I hope to do my part to help create accessible, easy to use spaces for everyone, regardless of their level of funtioning on a spectrum of abilities.
